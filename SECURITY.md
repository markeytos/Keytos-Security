## Scope
1) Unfortunately some XSS vulnerabilities cannot be fixed due to the APIs being called from other applications as well, to mitigate we have a strict CORS policy for browser access, and enable companies to set dual key approval for all administrative tasks. 
1) for any pentesting that might affect customer workloads we ask you to use our test site: https://sshmantest.azurewebsites.net/ please contact us at security@keytos.io to get access to our test tenant.
1) Phishing attacks and Malware attacks against Keytos Employees are strictly prohibited. 

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report any vulnerability to security@keytos.io.
